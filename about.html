Every Firm That Got Fined Had a Supervision System

They captured the violation perfectly. After it was sent. After clients saw it. After regulators found it. That's not compliance. That's evidence collection. Now AI is making it worse. Then everywhere language and regulation intersect. Teams move faster because they know they're protected. Compliance teams sleep better because problems get fixed at the source.
