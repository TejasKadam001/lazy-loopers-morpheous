Common Health Pathways

Hypertension (High Blood Pressure)

Symptoms often include frequent headaches and shortness of breath. Solutions involve targeted dietary changes, low-sodium intake, and strict medication management.

Type 2 Diabetes

Monitored through increased thirst, fatigue, and frequent urination. Solutions include comprehensive blood sugar tracking and insulin-sensitive exercise routines.

Anxiety & Depression

Presents as persistent sadness, extreme restlessness, or insomnia. Solutions integrate CBT, regulated clinical therapy, and monitored mindfulness practices.

Chronic Migraines

Characterized by intense throbbing pain and distinct light sensitivity. Solutions rely on identifying specific situational triggers and localized neurological therapies.
